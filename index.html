About the Mod

SMaTRS TOWERTIME! is a mod that focuses on change the game so it can be like Pizza Tower. in the other side, Lumi's Mod changes only the levels and only has the Lapping System from TOWERTIME.

New stuff: Level edits, "Pizza Time" gimmick at the end of a level, Lap 2, Pizzafying some levels like 1-8, And much more!

For more info about the original game, please visit the SMATRS Website

About (Vanilla)

Super Mario and the Rainbow Stars is a project that aims to bring the unique traits of the Paper Mario and Mario & Luigi series into a fun 2D platformer. Explore 7 different chapters, discover new partners and unique worlds, and stop Starnox's evil plans!

Features: New moves, cutscenes, 3D world map, tons of powerups, a 3 hp life system, partner system, original songs and more!

Story

One day, the Mario Bros were hanging out near their house until they suddenly received a letter from Peach. They've been invited for the Star Festival! Before that festival starts, they're planning to take a trip with a hot air balloon. Things turn out for the worse once something goes wrong...

Everyone gets scattered, and now it's up to Mario to find his friends, as well as retrieve the Rainbow Stars, which were scattered by the newly awakened Starnox. Mario must get these Rainbow Stars, otherwise the plan of plunging the world into darkness might succeed... Thankfully Mario will be accompanied by many different partners alongside his journey.

From a star kid to a ghostly spirit, and from a blooper pirate to the dark star itself, safe to say Mario will encounter many unique friends and foes on his adventure! This adventure will take you through the archipelago where Yoshis reside, an abstract forest inhabited by many different ghosts, a sky island which holds all 4 seasons at once and more!
